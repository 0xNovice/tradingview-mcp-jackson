────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  MOVING AVERAGE LINES (on price chart)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  WHITE line  (21 SMA)    — Primary entry/exit trigger. Price crossing above =
                            long signal. Price crossing below = exit/short signal.
                            The "Twisted Sister" line — most important line.

  YELLOW line (200 SMA)   — Macro bias line. THE key dividing line.
                            Close ABOVE 200 = Bull bias (green background).
                            Close BELOW 200 = Bear bias (red background).
                            Stop loss goes just below this line (200 × 0.999).

  TEAL line   (Smooth MA) — SMMA/RMA(50). Sam's "smooth moving average."
                            When price is ABOVE it = "Smooth Boost" territory,
                            extended price targets become possible (113-118% range).
                            Stop for extended targets goes below this line.

  RED line    (50 SMA)    — Intermediate structure / trend filter.

  BLUE line   (100 SMA)   — Deeper support reference.

  PURPLE line (800 SMA)   — Deep macro support. Long-term structure.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  BACKGROUND COLORS (chart backdrop)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  FAINT GREEN background  — Bull bias. Close is above the 200 SMA.
                            Only take LONG trades in this zone.

  FAINT RED background    — Bear bias. Close is below the 200 SMA.
                            Only take SHORT trades (or stay flat).

  BRIGHT GREEN bg (session) — London-NY Overlap (8am-12pm EST). Best session.
                               Highest liquidity, Sam's primary trading window.

  FAINT BLUE bg (session)  — London Session only (3am-8am EST, before NY opens).

  FAINT AQUA bg (session)  — New York Session only (12pm-5pm EST, after London closes).

  FAINT ORANGE bg          — Tokyo/Asia Session (if enabled). Lower priority.

  GRAY bg                  — Weekend. No trades. Markets are thin.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  SIGNAL SHAPES (plotted on price chart)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  TRIANGLE UP — YELLOW — text "FIRE"     = FIREWORKS ENTRY (STRONGEST)
    Condition: Bull bias (above 200) + price crossed ABOVE 21 SMA + the 200 SMA
               cross happened within the last 10 bars + in active session.
    Action:    Highest conviction long entry. Price just crossed the 200 and is
               now touching the 21. Fresh momentum. Enter long here.
               Stop: just below 200 SMA. Target: 3:1 R:R minimum.

  TRIANGLE UP — TEAL — text "SMB"        = SMOOTH BOOST ENTRY
    Condition: Bull bias + price is ABOVE the Smooth MA + touched 21 SMA from
               below + in session.
    Action:    Extended target setup. Same entry as pullback but with a higher
               price target possible (above the Smooth MA level = 113-118 zone).
               Sam calls this the "smooth boost" — price already showing strength.

  TRIANGLE UP — GREEN — text "21"        = STANDARD PULLBACK ENTRY
    Condition: Bull bias + price crossed above 21 SMA + in session.
               (Not a Fireworks or Smooth Boost setup — normal pullback.)
    Action:    Standard long entry. Price pulled back to the 21 SMA in an
               uptrend. Default R:R target is 3:1.

  TRIANGLE DOWN — RED — text "SHORT"     = SHORT ENTRY
    Condition: Bear bias (below 200 SMA) + price crossed BELOW 21 SMA + session.
    Action:    Short setup. Mirror of the long pullback but on the bear side.
               Cover when: price crosses back above 200 SMA, OR gapped below
               21 AND Stoch RSI is oversold.

  TRIANGLE DOWN — RED — text "OB"        = GAP + OVERBOUGHT FULL EXIT
    Condition: Price is 2.5%+ away from the 21 SMA AND Stoch RSI K and D both
               >= 80 (overbought).
    Action:    CLOSE ALL of the position. Both the gap AND overbought = best
               exit combination. Do not hold through this signal.

  TRIANGLE DOWN — ORANGE — text "GAP"    = PARTIAL EXIT (75%)
    Condition: Price has gapped 2.5%+ away from the 21 SMA (but Stoch RSI is
               NOT yet overbought — otherwise the OB signal fires instead).
    Action:    Take 75% of the position off the table. Keep 25% as a runner.
               The runner rides until: price returns to entry (breakeven), OR
               the OB signal fires, OR the Bear Flip kill switch triggers.
               Note: Gap % threshold is adjustable — Sam suggests tuning per TF:
                 4H chart   → 3-5% gap
                 15m chart  → 1.5-2% gap
                 5m chart   → 1% gap

  X CROSS — RED — text "BEAR"            = BEAR FLIP KILL SWITCH
    Condition: Price crossed BELOW the 200 SMA.
    Action:    CLOSE ALL LONG POSITIONS IMMEDIATELY. No questions asked.
               Macro bias has flipped to bearish. This is the kill switch.
               Sam: if price breaks the 200, the trend is over for longs.

  DIAMOND — TEAL (tiny) — text "SMT"     = SMOOTH MA CROSS
    Condition: Price just crossed ABOVE the Smooth MA (teal line).
    Action:    Not an entry signal by itself — a context signal. This tells you
               the Smooth Boost entry type is now available IF price pulls back
               to the 21 SMA. Extended targets are now in play above the smooth
               MA level. Watch for SMB triangle to appear on next 21 SMA touch.

  FIBONACCI DOTS (price levels plotted as dot lines)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  ORANGE dots — Fib 0.786  — Deep retracement level (78.6% pullback of swing range).
                              Last major support before the swing low is taken out.
                              If price drops to this level, the trend structure is
                              being severely tested. A bounce here is the final
                              "last chance" entry before the setup is invalidated.

  LIME dots   — Fib 0.618  — Golden Pocket LOW. The start of the high-probability
                              entry zone. This is where the majority of healthy
                              pullbacks find support in a strong uptrend.

  LIME dots   — Fib 0.650  — Golden Pocket HIGH. The top of the Golden Pocket zone.
                              Together with 0.618, these two lime dot lines form the
                              "GP Zone" — Sam's preferred area for 21 SMA pullback
                              entries when they align with the GP zone simultaneously.

  Swing lookback = 50 bars (adjustable). The fibs are drawn from the highest high
  to the lowest low over that lookback period.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  PVSRA VOLUME CANDLE COLORS (ported from TR_PVSRA_VS — Traders Reality)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  HOW PVSRA WORKS:
  The volume of each bar is compared to a 10-bar SMA of volume AND the
  "spread × volume" product (candle range × volume = total participation).
  This identifies when institutional money is active (vectors) vs when
  retail/low-participation is trading (regular bars).

  TWO VECTOR TIERS:

  ── RG VECTOR (Red/Green — STRONGEST signal) ──
    Condition: volume >= 200% of 10-bar avg volume
               OR (high - low) × volume >= highest of that product over last 10 bars
    Meaning:   Extreme volume OR the highest candle-spread participation in 10 bars.
               These are the bars where institutions/whales are most active.

    LIME GREEN candle = RG Vector + Bull bar (close >= open)
    → Strong institutional BUYING. High conviction. Look for entries in direction.
    → On a 21 SMA touch: this is the ideal entry confirmation.

    RED candle = RG Vector + Bear bar (close < open)
    → Strong institutional SELLING. High conviction. Watch out — may signal reversal.
    → On a 21 SMA break below: confirms the downward pressure.

  ── BV VECTOR (Blue/Violet — SECONDARY signal) ──
    Condition: volume >= 150% of 10-bar avg volume (but NOT an RG vector)
    Meaning:   Elevated but not extreme volume. Above-average institutional
               participation. A supporting signal, not as strong as RG.

    BLUE candle = BV Vector + Bull bar
    → Elevated buying, secondary confirmation. Decent entry bar signal.

    FUCHSIA (violet/magenta) candle = BV Vector + Bear bar
    → Elevated selling, secondary. Watch if price is at key levels.

  ── REGULAR BARS (no vector) ──
    LIGHT GRAY candle (#999999) = Regular Bull bar (close >= open, low volume)
    → Normal volume upbar. No institutional footprint. Retail/noise activity.

    DARK GRAY candle (#4d4d4d) = Regular Bear bar (close < open, low volume)
    → Normal volume downbar. No institutional footprint. Retail/noise activity.

  READING PVSRA IN CONTEXT:
    • Entry on 21 SMA touch with a LIME candle → very high conviction long
    • Entry on 21 SMA touch with a BLUE candle → good confirmation, take it
    • Entry on 21 SMA touch with GRAY candle  → marginal, wait for next bar or skip
    • RED/FUCHSIA candle at a resistance level → potential reversal warning
    • RED candle breaking below 200 SMA       → bear flip confirmed, close all

  Stop (below 200) — Current stop loss price (200 SMA × 0.999)

                  Red = already at or past the partial exit threshold

  Session       — Current trading session. "LDN-NY (BEST)" = prime time
  Volume        — Current PVSRA vector state + volume ratio vs 10-bar avg
                  (e.g., "RG VECTOR Bull (2.3x)" = RG vector at 2.3× average)
  Thu Risk      — CAUTION on Thursdays (Sam flags these as higher risk days)
